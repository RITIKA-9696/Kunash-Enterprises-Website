Our Story

Kunash Enterprises is a company that provides high quality refurbished and new laptop and desktop, as well as comprehensive AMC services. With a focus on customer satisfaction, Kunash enterprises is dedicated to providing top notch products and services at affordable price. In addition to selling laptops, we also offer a variety of services, such as laptop repair, data recovery, and virus removal. We are also authorized dealers for many of the leading laptop brands, so we can provide you with warranty support for your laptop. In addition to sales, we also offer a variety of laptop repair and maintenance services. Our certified technicians can fix any problem with your laptop, from a broken screen to a virus infection. We also offer offer regular maintenance services, such as cleaning and tune-ups, to help keep your laptop running smoothly.
